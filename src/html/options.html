Gammaloid Configuration

WARNING. DO NOT USE IF PHOTOSENSITIVE. GAMMALOID USES HIGH FREQUENCY STROBES.

Gamma frequency entrainment has been linked to changes in amyloid load in terms of attenuating Alzheimer's-disease-associated pathology.

This Chrome extension causes webpages in your browser to change brightness at given frequencies through an overlay on the screen.

The efficacy or safety of this tool has not been evaluated by anyone. Use at your own risk. This tool is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease.
